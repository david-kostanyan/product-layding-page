REPAIR AND MAINTENANCE OF AUTOMATIC GEAR

Automatic transmission is a multi-speed transmission that does not require gear shifting while driving. Automatic transmission is the most technologically advanced unit of a modern car, which consumes a huge amount of electrical, mechanical, hydraulic and electronic components, provides a drive of the car with maximum comfort at minimum fuel consumption.

ENGINE REPAIR AND SERVICE

On average, gasoline internal combustion engines are designed for 300 thousand km. The resource of diesel engines is 400 thousand kilometers and more. There are successful engines, there are not very successful ones. But much depends on the nature of operation, quality and timeliness of car maintenance. Sooner or later, engine diagnostics and repairs are required. Overhaul is understood as the process of removing the internal combustion engine from the vehicle for subsequent complete disassembly. Automotive craftsmen inspect the cylinder block, cylinder head, crankshaft and piston group for defects. Make a conclusion and establish a list of repairs.

REPAIR AND MAINTENANCE OF THE UNDERCARRIAGE

The chassis is one of the loaded systems of the vehicle. With the movement of the car, forces appear that act on parts and mechanisms. Wear and tear of running car components leads to the appearance of noise, vibrations, comfort and safety deteriorates. Running gear diagnostics is required even if you do not feel discomfort and do not see the reasons for contacting a service center.
